Objective

To provide safe, stable, and nurturing shelter for homeless, abandoned, and vulnerable children through community-supported housing sponsorships and donations.

Background

Too many children grow up without the security of home. Reflect Children Center offers more than shelter—it offers love, dignity, and opportunity. The "House a Child" campaign seeks to extend this mission by creating more homes and more chances for brighter tomorrows.

What the Campaign Is About

  • Raise funds and resources to buy safe housing units.
  • Sponsor individual children's accommodation and needs.
  • Promote community involvement in child welfare.
  • Support long-term care, counseling, and education access.

Target Goals

  • House 100+ children within 12 months.
  • Raise UGX 50M+ (~USD 13,000) in 6 months.
  • Gain 50+ monthly sponsors and long-term partners.
  • Establish housing clusters for safe living environments.

Target Audience

  • Local and international donors
  • Community and religious groups
  • Goodwill ambassadors and influencers
  • NGOs and government agencies

Key Deliverables

  • New housing units and dormitory clusters
  • Personalized sponsorship kits
  • Donor and impact reports
  • Branded campaign videos, posters, and brochures

Communication Channels

We amplify our message through social media, outreach drives, influencer advocacy, and crowdfunding platforms to create meaningful engagement.

Campaign Motto

“A roof is just the start — together, we build a future.”

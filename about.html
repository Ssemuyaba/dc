We Are R.UG

Donate

Reflect Divine Compassion Int is a faith inspired initiative dedicated to integrating spirituality and social work to provide holistic support to children, single mothers, disabled people in the community. Guided by values of love ,compassion, kindness and service the organisation seeks to address both the spiritual and material needs of vulnerable individuals fostering healing, empowerment and transformation in their lives.

Our Mission

To reflect the love of God through practical acts of Kindness and spiritual guidance by emppowering disabled people, single mothers,and naturing Children. The organisation works to create resilient families and thriving Communities.

Our Vission.

We envision a world where every child, mother or disabled person, regardless of their nature , experiences the unconditional love of God and is equipped to live dignity, love, hope and purpose in the community.

Tells us we being transformed as we behold and reflect the Lord like a mirror.

Our Values.

Kindness In Life

Small gestures suchas a smile, kind words can all together have a ripple effect brightening someone's day and encouraging them to do the same.

Compasionate Culture

Compassion isn't just an individual practice, its the foundation of a just and equitable society.It influences communities to show care for the vulnerable and disadvantaged and calls for collective responsibilty for global challenges such as luck of spiritual connectivity, poverty in the community and environmental changes.


What We Believe.

Power Of Compassion

With compassion in the society there is unavoidable healing to relationships which are God's first Church, forgiveness , empathy, kindness, reconciliation and results into communal bonds.

The Power to Reflect

Reflecting is a tranformative practice that allows us to pause, look inward and gain clarity about our thoughts, actions and experiences. When combined with compassion, reflecting becomes a powerfull tool for personal growth, deeper connections and meaningfull changes in the Wolrd.

Inspiring Action

Society actions are always pron to inspiration towards the young generations thus each action a society takes, we believe followed by compassion and the reflecting act can be a gear that drives socail changes, motivate people to address injustices and create systems that prioritizes human diginity and well being.

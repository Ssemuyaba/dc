MushRoom Farming

The mushroom-growing project empowers individuals by teaching sustainable farming techniques and providing the resources needed to cultivate mushrooms. This initiative focuses on low-cost, high-yield production, making it accessible even to those with limited means. By producing a highly nutritious and in-demand crop, participants can generate a reliable income to support their families. The program also educates beneficiaries on value addition, such as drying and packaging mushrooms for better market appeal. This initiative targets single mothers and disadvantaged individuals, offering them a pathway to self-reliance.

Reflect Divine provides starter kits, ongoing mentorship, and market linkages to ensure the project's sustainability. Beyond income, the project contributes to food security and improved nutrition in the community. Participants gain confidence as they learn valuable agricultural skills, creating a ripple effect of empowerment. This environmentally friendly initiative also promotes the use of organic materials and waste recycling, making it sustainable and impactful.
